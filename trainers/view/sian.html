About Me:

Despite starting running 'late' in life (at the age of 19), running has become my passion and way of life. I quickly became a competitive club level athlete and, in only my second year of running, was selected to represent North Wales in various cross country competitions and continue to do so today. My main focus, however, lies on the track, with 800ms and 1500ms being my specialisms. I have experience in various levels of competition, with personal best times of 2.30 in the 800ms and 4.59 in the 1500ms. On the road I enjoy running 5K races, with a personal best time of 19.13. Rapid improvements in my performances led to me receiving my athletics' club award of 'most improved female athlete' of 2010.
As a primary school teacher, I have experience of coaching children in athletics, both in extra curricular clubs and at my athletics club. When coaching I aim to provide enjoyment and passion for the sport, as well as helping people to achieve their goals.
